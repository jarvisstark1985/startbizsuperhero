What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you on return visits, remember your preferences, and understand how you interact with our platform. Cookies may be either "persistent" (stored until you delete them) or "session" (deleted when you close your browser).

We use cookies to enhance your experience, provide analytics, and personalize content. By continuing to use StartBiz SuperHero, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

  • • Session Cookies: Maintain your login session and security preferences
  • • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • • CSRF Protection: Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how you use our website by collecting anonymized data about your browsing behavior. This information is used to improve our services and user experience.

How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can typically:

  • • View what cookies have been set on your device
  • • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • • Block cookies from specific websites or all websites
  • • Set preferences for blocking third-party cookies

Important: Disabling essential cookies may prevent certain features of our website from functioning properly. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best experience. However, you can safely disable analytics and functional cookies if you prefer.
