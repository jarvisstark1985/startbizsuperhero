Introduction

TheAISuperHeroes ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the StartBizSuperHero platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

By accessing and using StartBizSuperHero, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Service.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, organization details, and profile information. This information is necessary to set up and manage your account.

Payment Information

We process subscription payments ($14.99/month) through Stripe. We do not store full credit card details on our servers. Stripe handles all payment processing and complies with PCI DSS standards. We retain billing address and the last four digits of your card for record-keeping.

Usage Analytics

We collect data about how you use the Service, including features accessed, time spent, agent interactions, API calls, and search queries. This helps us improve the platform and provide better user experience.

API Usage Data

When you use our API, we log request details, response times, error messages, and usage patterns. This data is used to monitor service health, prevent abuse, and optimize performance.

Agent Interaction Data

We collect information about agents you discover, use, and deploy, including configurations, execution logs, and interaction results. This enables us to provide analytics, recommendations, and marketplace features.

Technical Information

We automatically collect information about your device and connection, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited. This helps us diagnose issues and enhance security.

Communication Data

If you contact our support team, we retain your messages, attachments, and correspondence to resolve issues and improve our support quality.

How We Use Your Information

Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the StartBizSuperHero platform and its features.

Account Management: To manage your subscription, process payments, send invoices, and handle account requests.

Analytics & Insights: To analyze usage patterns, create reports, and understand how users interact with the Service.

Personalization: To customize your experience, recommend relevant agents, and tailor features to your preferences.

Communication: To send service updates, security alerts, billing notifications, and respond to your inquiries.

Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent unauthorized access, abuse, and fraudulent activity.

Legal Compliance: To comply with laws, regulations, and legal processes.

Product Development: To research, develop, and test new features and improvements.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access sensitive information, and access is logged and monitored.

Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and address vulnerabilities.

Third-Party Security: We vet service providers and ensure they maintain appropriate security standards.

However, no security system is completely foolproof. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.
